About Us

Vera's Cleaning was established in 2015. Vera worked as a house cleaner during her teenage years and after she got married, she decided to start her own business. Now in 2020, her business has grown into a staff of 5 to help clean all her customer's homes. She is excited to add more customers to her ever growing business and hopes to hear from you soon!
