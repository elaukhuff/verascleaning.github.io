Vera offers many cleaning services to keep your home sparkling!

Service Description
Dusting We dust all surfaces, furniture, and decor in the home as well as baseboards and ceiling fans. We also windex all mirrors.
Vacuuming & Mopping We vacuum and/or sweep all flooring in the house and wipe down all the floors by hand.
Kitchen We wipe down all counters and cabinets, clean the microwave and stove, and scrub the sink. We also take the trash out.
Bathroom We wipe down all counters and cabinets, clean the sink and windex the mirrors, scrub the toilets and showers/bathtubs.

Cleaning Packages

Package Description
Basic Cleaning This includes dusting, vacuuming, and mopping of all rooms, as well as cleaning the kitchen and all bathrooms
Basic Cleaning + Basement This includes everything listed in the basic cleaning package, as well as dusting, vacuuming and/or mopping the basement
Custom This also includes everything in the basic cleaning package as well as any special requests you might have, such as cleaning windows, wiping down walls, or vacuuming couches!
